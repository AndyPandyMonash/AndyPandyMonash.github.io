What Are The Top Uni's?

The top universities in the world are all pretty well known. The top three being Oxford, Harvard and Cambrdige. The distribution of the top 100 universities is also very intresting. With almost half of the top 100 uni's being located in America with England coming in second place. Autralia's top three universities are the University of Melbourne, Monash and The University of Sydney.

Which Counrties Have The Best Uni's?

As mentioned almost half of the worlds top universites are located within the USA. This is due to the states being much larger in population than most other top contenders but also due to the fact that they simply have some of the best universities in the world when ranked by score. In second place is the UK with 13% and Germany in third place with 9%. However, as seen in the doughnut chart, there is a wide distribution of countries which have at least one or more universities within the top 100. Meaning that no matter where you are located, there is a high chance that you can get a world class education.

How Important Is Teaching In Determining University Score?

As seen in the bubble chart on the left there is a strong linear trend between "Teaching Score" and "Overall Score".
